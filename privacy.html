🌙 Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Moonphase Tracker ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Location Search Data - The city names you search are sent to Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) to retrieve coordinates. We do not store these searches.
  • No Personal Data - We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiable information.
  • No Cookies - We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that follow you across the web.
  • No IP Logging - We do not log IP addresses or track your visit history.

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing accurate astronomical calculations for your location
  • Displaying moon phase data relevant to your geographic position
  • Improving the accuracy of our calculations and features

4. Data Storage & Retention

We do not store user data. Location searches are processed in real-time and not retained. All calculations happen in your browser's memory and are not saved to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Nominatim (OpenStreetMap): We use this free, open-source service to convert city names to geographic coordinates. When you search for a location, your search is sent to Nominatim's servers. Please refer to Nominatim's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Content Delivery Networks (CDN): We use jsDelivr to load JavaScript libraries. Please refer to their privacy policies for details on how they handle requests.

6. Security

Browser-Based Processing: Most of our application runs locally in your browser, meaning calculations and data processing never leave your device. Only location searches are sent to third parties.

HTTPS: All communications use HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal data and do not store personal information, there is no user data to:

  • Access or download
  • Delete or modify
  • Restrict or port

If you have concerns about how third-party services (like Nominatim) handle your data, please contact them directly.

8. Children's Privacy

Moonphase Tracker is safe for all ages. We do not collect any data from children or anyone else. There are no account systems, registrations, or age restrictions.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the information provided on our main website.
