Today's Moon Position by Hour

This hourly moon altitude chart shows how high the moon will be in the sky throughout today, measured in degrees above the horizon. Positive values indicate the moon is visible (above the horizon), while negative values mean it's below the horizon. The peak of the curve shows the moon's highest point in the sky (transit time). This information is essential for photographers planning lunar photography sessions, astronomers tracking lunar positions, and anyone interested in moon observation and celestial tracking.
